Homo erectus


TYPE SPECIMEN




Name/Nickname

"Java Man"


Discovery

1891 by Eugene Dubois


Site

Trinil on the banks of the Solo River


Time of Existence

1.89 mya to 143 kya


Paleoenvironment

Moist grasslands with open woodlands


Primitive Traits

Brain case is long, low, and thick-walled, strong browridge


Derived Traits

Modern human-like body proportions - elongated legs and shorter arms compared to the torso
