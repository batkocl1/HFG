Type Specimen


"

Name/Nickname

"Lucy AL 288-1"


Discovery

1974 by Donald Johnson


Site

Hadar, Ethiopia


Time of Existence

3.8 - 2.9 mya


Paleoenvironment

Somewhat dry, possibly open, wooded, and bushland areas


Primitive Traits

Small brain (350-400cc), low cranium with inferior portion, prognathic face, longer arm to leg ratio, big toe grasping capabilities - long finger bones - curved phalanges


Derived Traits

Anterior teeth reduced, diastma reduced, posterior teeth large
