Sahelanthropus tchadensis


TYPE SPECIMEN




Nickname

"Toumai"


Discovery

2001 by Michel Brunet


Site

Toros Menalla - Sahel Region of Chad


Time of Existence

7.0 to 6.0 mya


Paleoenvironment

Well-watered woodland


Primitive Traits

Prominent brow ridge, sagittal crest, small cranial capacity (smaller than chimps), vertical/sloping face, elongated skull, prognathism


Derived Traits

Small canines, small center part of face, foramen magnum located more inferior on the bottom of the skull
