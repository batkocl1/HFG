Homo habilis


TYPE SPECIMEN




Name/Nickname

OH-7 or "Jonny's Child"


Discovery

1960 by Jonathan Leakey


Site

Olduvai Gorge (Bed 1, FLK-NN) - Tanzania


Time of Existence

1.75 mya


Paleoenvironment

Predominantly grassland environment


Primitive Traits

large anterior teeth, large canines, long arms, moderately-prognathic face, short legs, prominent brow ridge, slightly curved finger bones


Derived Traits

retreating chin region, larger cranial capacity, smaller teeth and face than australopiths, rounded jaw, reduced prognathism
