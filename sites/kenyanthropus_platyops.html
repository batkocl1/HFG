Kenyanthropus platyops


TYPE SPECIMEN




Name/Nickname

KNM-WT 40,000 or "Flat-Faced Man"


Discovery

1999 by Meave Leakey


Site

Lomekwi Site - West of Lake Turkana - Kenya


Time of Existence

3.5 mya


Paleoenvironment

Wooded grassland environment - similar to afarensis


Primitive Traits

Brain size (430cc), facial prognathism


Derived Traits

Cheek teeth with thick enamel, toe bone suggest bipedalism, flat face across nasal region
