Ardipithecus ramidus


Type Specimen


"

Name/Nickname

ARA-VP-6/500 or "Ardi"


Discovery

1994 by Tim White


Site

Ethiopian Badlands, Village of Aramis (Middle Awash)


Time of Existence

4.4 mya


Paleoenvironment

Heavily forested flood plain


Primitive Traits

Divergent big toe, ape-like dentition, canine and incisor to post-canine dental ratio is large, enamel is thin, pelvis still equipped for tree-climbing


Derived Traits

Canines are small, foramen magnum anteriorly placed, less sexually dimorphic, pelvic modified for bipedality
