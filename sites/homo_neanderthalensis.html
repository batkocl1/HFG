Homo neanderthalensis


TYPE SPECIMEN




Name/Nickname

Neanderthal 1


Discovery

1858 by Johann Carl Fuhlrott


Site

Feldhoffer Grotto - Neandertal - Germany


Time of Existence

200 - 40 kya


Paleoenvironment

Cold, Ice-Age enivornment


Primitive Traits

Long and low vault, double-arched browridge, prognathic midface, occipital bun, absent chin, large central incisors, overall robust build


Derived Traits

large maxillary sinsues, large rounded orbits, large nasal apeture, medial projections inside nasal cavity, retromolar space, smaller cheek teeth, very long clavicles, massive head, broad-tipped fingers, dorsally rotated pelvis
